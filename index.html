About me

My name is Katrine Bern and I am 25 years old. I am currently doing my 5th semester of Global Business Engineering, SW at VIA University College. When I am not studying or attending school, I love to knit, work with clay and recently picked up sewing to (hopefully) expand my crafting skillset. Originally, I come from Vejle where I also attended High School. During my gap years, I was a Process Specialist at a warehouse for one of the biggest clothing retailers in Denmark. In this role, I was in charge of the night shift making sure orders were shipped out correctly and on time. This gave me an insight into how poorly a work environment can be affected from unqualified leadership and rough implementations of IT solutions where it is prevalent that end users were not kept in mind during development. I thought to myself "this cannot be the most effective way to implement and launch new IT solutions", and decided to find out for myself - which is why I decided to study Global Business Engineering.
